Scope

This policy applies to information collected through our website, forms, demos, email communications, and during the course of our services. It does not cover data processed under separate contracts where we act as a processor on behalf of a client — those arrangements are governed by the applicable agreement.

Information We Collect

We collect two categories of information: data you provide directly and technical/usage data collected automatically.

Data you provide

  • Contact and identity details submitted via contact forms, proposals, or email (name, business email, phone, company)
  • Business information provided for demos or assessments (industry, revenue band, use-case descriptions)
  • Support or correspondence messages and any files you upload to facilitate our work

Automatically collected data

  • Device and browser information, IP address, and approximate geographic region
  • Usage metrics such as pages visited, timestamps, and interaction events
  • Cookies and analytics identifiers (see Cookies section)

AI-Specific Data Practices

Because we build and deploy AI systems, we include specific practices to address risks and transparency around AI:

  • How we use input data: When you provide information to an AI demo, prototype, or production system, we use that input to generate outputs for the requested service. Inputs may be logged for debugging, quality monitoring, and to improve our models unless explicitly excluded in a client contract.
  • Model training: We do not use customer-identifiable data to train our core public models unless we have explicit consent or a contractual agreement that permits such use. Aggregated or de-identified data may be used to improve services where legal and appropriate.
  • Outputs and liability: AI-generated outputs may be incomplete, inaccurate, or biased. Outputs are provided for convenience and do not replace professional judgment. We are not responsible for decisions you make based on AI outputs unless covered by a separate service agreement.
  • De-identification and minimization: We follow data minimization principles: only data necessary for the service is collected, and where possible we remove or redact direct identifiers before storage or secondary use.

How We Use Information

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries and provide the requested services or demos
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and AI systems
  • To monitor and investigate technical issues, security incidents, and abuse
  • To send administrative communications and service-related notices

Third-Party Services & Processors

We may share personal data with trusted third-party service providers who help deliver our services (for example, hosting, analytics, email delivery, scheduling, and payment processors). These providers are contractually required to protect data and use it only as instructed.

Where we integrate third-party AI or cloud providers, those providers may process data on our behalf; their privacy and security practices are controlled by their own policies and agreements. We recommend reviewing those providers' documentation when appropriate.

International Transfers

Our services and providers may process data in countries outside your location. When personal data is transferred internationally, we use contractual safeguards and standard contractual clauses where required to provide appropriate protections.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Typical retention periods:

  • Contact and inquiry records: up to 3 years after last contact
  • Client engagement data: duration of engagement plus 5 years (or per contract)
  • Logs and analytics: retained according to our provider policies and aggregated where possible

Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encrypted transport (HTTPS), access controls, logging, and periodic security reviews. However, no system is completely secure; promptly contact us if you suspect a security incident.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond within applicable legal timeframes.
